About Me

I am now looking for a postdoctoral position in high-performance system and architecture design!

My name is Jiajun Li (李家军). I am a Computer Science Ph.D. candidate in Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences, where I am a member of Architecture for Data Analytics & Processing Technology Group (ADAPT) and advised by Prof. Xiaowei Li. I earned my bachelor's degree in Control Science and Engineering from Tsinghua University.

Research Interests

My research broadly falls into general areas of high-performance and energy-efficient computing . More specifically, I am interested in: (1) Domain-specific accelerators; (2) Parallel programming models and runtime; (3) Performance optimization and modeling.
My most recent research work is focusing on deep learning accelerators.
